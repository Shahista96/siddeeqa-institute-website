WOMEN'S
WELLNESS CENTER

Siddeeqa Institute is excited to launch its Women’s Wellness Center, aimed at the holistic wellness of Muslim women.

We believe that while the most important aspect of our existence is our spiritual self and its health and development, this is not possible without the soundness of the rest, and holistic healing and nurturing are necessary to fulfil the purpose of our creation and to strengthen our connection with our Creator, and hence actualize our true potential.

Aspects of Holistic Wellness
Physical Wellness Includes proper nutrition, adequate exercise, timely identification of diseases, protection from bodily harm, sleep management, weight management; menstruation, birth control, pregnancy, childbirth and post-natal care, etc.
Mental Wellness Includes mindfulness, self-awareness, positive self-image, positive thinking, time management, planning, productivity, identifying the need for mental health support, coping with adversity, etc.
Emotional Wellness Includes accurate identification and appropriate expression of emotions, self-esteem building, optimism, stress management, etc.
Intellectual Wellness Includes opinion formation in an era of information overload, sifting between reliable and unreliable sources, critical thinking, creativity, etc.
Social Wellness Includes building healthy relationships, identifying and managing toxic relationships, communication skills, community-building, social activism, conflict resolution, etc.
Environmental Wellness Includes living in harmony with nature, minimizing harm to the environment, protecting ourselves from the impact of environmental disasters, etc.
Financial Wellness Includes career counselling, discussion of work from home options, ensuring shari'ah compliancy of small businesses, budgeting, saving, investment.
Spiritual Wellness Includes finding a higher purpose, strengthening and deepening our relationship with the Creator ﷻ, learning about and acting in accordance with His Will, being concerned about the Hereafter, mindfulness in worship, etc.

Basic Expectations From Students
  • 4 hours of class time per week
  • Assignments on weekends requiring 1 to 2 hours

SUPPORT
GROUPS

These are small groups of women who are facing similar challenges, each led by a trained facilitator, in which the women share their experiences in a structured way, and in doing so benefit themselves and others. Some of the issues around which support groups may be based include:

• Recent conversion to Islam
• Being a minority in a non-Muslim country
• Struggles in acting upon deen
• Challenges in co-educational settings
• Challenges in the workplace
• Settling into married life
• Parenting
• Loss of a loved one

​Since one support group will not include more than 15 women, admission will be given to those who most direly need it, and priority will be given to Siddeeqa Institute students.

Please contact our Support Group Coordinator if you wish to be notified via Whatsapp when a support group is offered.

Disclaimer: These support groups will not handle life-threatening issues like addictions, substance abuse, eating disorders, suicidal tendencies, or clinical conditions like depression or anxiety; they will also not be a substitute for medication and therapy.

The purpose and benefits of support groups include:
First, talking about concerns and giving voice to associated thoughts and feelings in order to more clearly define the problem and gain greater self-awareness.
Second, eliminating a sense of isolation and gaining strength and hope.
Third, learning strategies for dealing with the problems at hand, and picking up mental and emotional coping mechanisms, from each other and from the group facilitator.

REVERT SUPPORT
SERVICES

Siddeeqa Institute provides support to reverted Muslimahs by offering the following services:
  • Individual and group classes to learn how to read the Blessed Qur'an
  • Short courses in basic Islamic knowledge
  • A fiqh question and answer WhatsApp group of reverted Muslimahs only, in which they can freely and comfortably ask any questions related to Islamic rulings
  • Facilitator-led support groups for new Muslimahs

If you are interested in availing any of these services, please contact our Revert Support Services Coordinator.

WORKSHOPS &
LECTURES

Siddeeqa Institute's Women’s Wellness Center will offer a wide range of workshops and lectures throughout the year on matters related to women’s holistic wellness. These sessions will be conducted by experts in their respective fields, and we will be collaborating with highly qualified and committed women around the world to bring you these programs.​

To register for a short course, workshop, or lecture, please message the number given on the relevant poster, or contact the Women's Wellness Center Coordinator.
